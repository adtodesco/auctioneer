Welcome to the Don Orsillo Open free agency auction! The DOO free agency is done via sealed-bid auctions where the highest bid equals the players total salary. Free agency takes place here at thedooauction.com and results will be imported into fantrax at the end of free agency.

Auction Rules

  • Managers can nominate and bid on any player in the player pool with their bid equaling the total salary paid to the player if they win the auction.
  • If a manager has match rights for a player they will get 24 hours to accept or decline the match option once the auction has closed. A manager has match rights if their player is on a minor league or free agency contract (i.e. if that player was not an in-season waiver claim). Check the 2024 FA match eligibility sheet for a list of match eligble players this year.
  • If there is ever a tie for the highest bid, a tiebreaker order is used to break the tie. When a manager wins an auction via the tiebreaker they are moved to the bottom of the tiebreaker order. The current tiebreaker order can be found in the tiebreaker order page.
  • Every Sunday at 5pm ET and Wednesday at 8pm ET a round of 28 player auctions will close (28 players per round * 2 rounds per week = 56 players per week).
  • Each round has a nomination period that starts 7 days before and ends 4 days before the round of player auctions closes.
  • Managers can nominate up to two players in each round during the nomination period. When managers make a nomination they also make an initial bid - this initial bid can be updated but it cannot be removed.
  • Managers can bid on any and all auctions between the time the nomination is made and the time that auction closes.
  • The highest bid wins the auction. The highest bid becomes the minimum total salary.

Assigning Contracts

Once a player has been won in auction the player will appear on their teams roster and the manager can sign them to a contract. Managers can modify their players' contracts through the end of free agency on March 10. Contracts must meet the minimum contracts specified in the salary cap and contracts sheet. Only valid contracts will be given as options in the signing page.

  • Note that the annual salary x contract length may end up being slightly greater than the total salary because weâ€™re always rounding up the annual salary value when the total salary cannot be divided evenly. For example - if you win a bid with a $82 total salary, your contract options are 1-year at $82, 2-years at $41 or 3 years at $28. In the 3 year option case the annual salary of $27.33 ($82 / 3 = $27.33) is rounded up to $28, and so the total salary ends up being $28 * 3 = $84. Therefore your auction bid is technically the minimum total salary depending on whether the annual salary needs to be rounded up. You may (or may not) want to take this into consideration when making your bids.

Reminders

  • Going forward only players signed to 2+ year contracts will be match eligible.
  • Once free agency closes the waiver wire opens back up which you can use to fill any remaining open roster spots. Waiver wire claims process on Sunday nights and - similarly to the free agency auction - the highest bidder wins the claim and the player assigned a contract with the bid equaling their total salary.

Timeline

  • Sunday, February 4 @ 7:15 pm ET - The nomination period for round 1 auctions opens. You have 3 days to nominate up to 2 players in this round.
  • Wednesday, February 7 @ 9:15 pm ET - The nomination period for round 2 auctions opens. You have 3 days to nominate up to 2 players in this round.
  • Sunday, February 11 @ 5:00 pm ET - round 1 auctions begin to close. Auctions close every 5 minutes between 5pm ET and 7:15pm ET.
  • Sunday, February 11 @ 7:15 pm ET - The nomination period for round 3 auctions opens. You have 3 days to nominate up to 2 players in this round.
  • Wednesday, February 14 @ 7:00 pm ET - round 2 auctions begin to close. Auctions close every 5 minutes between 7pm ET and 9:15 pm ET.
  • Wednesday, February 14 @ 9:15 pm ET - The nomination period for round 4 auctions opens. You have 3 days to nominate up to 2 players in this round.
  • Repeat every Sunday/Wednesday through round 8 closing on Wednesday, March 6th.
  • Wednesday, March 10 @ Midnight ET - All free agent contracts must be finalized. The final auction results will be downloaded and rosters will be updated in Fantrax over the next few days.
  • Wednesday, March 20 - Thursday, March 21 - SDP v LAD games in Korea.
  • Thursday, March 28 - MLB opening day.
