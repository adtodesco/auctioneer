Auction Rules

  • There are six auction rounds that close every Sunday at 6 pm ET with 28 players per round.
  • The nomination period for each auction round runs from Monday night through Thursday night.
  • Managers can nominate at least two players per round. If the round has not been filled by Thursday then managers can nominated a third player.
  • All managers can bid on all nominated players. The nominating manager must have at least the minimum bid for any player they nominated.
  • The winning bid is revealed when the auction closes. This bid amount becomes the player's total salary.
  • If there's a tie, a tiebreaker order is used and the winning manager moves to the bottom of the order. The tiebreaker order page shows the current order.
  • Managers with match rights have 24 hours after the auction closes to accept or decline the option to re-sign the player. Managers get match rights for players coming off of a 2+ year contract.
  • Once a player is won in auction, they will appear on the team's roster and the manager can sign them to a contract.
  • Contracts must meet the minimum requirements specified in the salary cap and contracts sheet. Only valid contracts will be available on the signing page.
